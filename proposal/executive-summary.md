# Executive Summary

**RFP #:** MC-2026-0417 — Inventory Dashboard Modernization
**Submitted by:** Accenture
**Date:** April 28, 2026

---

Meridian Components operates a mission-critical inventory dashboard that your operations team relies on daily to track stock levels, orders, supplier spend, and demand forecasts across three warehouses. The current system, delivered by a previous vendor in 2024, is functional — but incomplete. The Reports module carries unresolved defects, there is no automated test coverage (which has blocked your IT team from approving changes), and the documentation left behind is insufficient for safe ongoing maintenance.

We understand this isn't just a technical cleanup. Your VP Operations needs a Restocking capability that doesn't exist yet. Your Tokyo warehouse team is working in English-only interfaces. And your IT team needs the confidence that changes won't break what's already working before they can greenlight any of it.

**Our approach is structured around four phases over eight weeks:**

1. **Onboarding & Architecture Review** (Weeks 1–2) — We perform a full architecture review of the existing codebase, deliver current-state documentation to your IT team, and establish the test framework. This gives both teams a shared understanding of what we're working with.

2. **Reports Remediation** (Weeks 3–4) — We conduct a comprehensive audit of the Reports module, identify and resolve all defects — filter behavior, internationalization gaps, console errors, data pattern inconsistencies — and deliver end-to-end test coverage for this module.

3. **Restocking Feature** (Weeks 5–7) — We design and build the new Restocking view: an intelligent recommendation engine that factors in current stock levels, demand forecasts, and an operator-supplied budget ceiling to generate purchase order suggestions.

4. **Enhancements & Handoff** (Week 8) — We address the desired items — UI refresh, extended internationalization, dark mode — and deliver a clean handoff package with full documentation and test coverage.

We've structured this engagement for **predictable delivery with clear milestones** at each phase boundary. Your team will have visibility into progress at every stage, and the test-first approach means your IT team can validate changes with confidence from Week 1.

We look forward to the opportunity to partner with Meridian Components on this engagement.
