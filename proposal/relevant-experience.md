## Engagement 1 — Warehouse Analytics Platform Remediation

**Client:** North American industrial parts distributor (5 warehouses, ~$40M revenue)
**Duration:** 10 weeks
**Stack:** Vue 3, Python/FastAPI, PostgreSQL

The client had an existing warehouse analytics platform built by an offshore team that was abandoned mid-development. We were brought in to stabilize the application, resolve 20+ known defects in reporting and filtering modules, and deliver automated test coverage to unblock the client's internal IT team from maintaining the system.

**Results:**
- All defects resolved within the first 4 weeks
- Delivered 85% E2E test coverage using Playwright
- Reduced page load times by 40% through query optimization
- Platform successfully handed off to client's internal team with full architecture documentation

**Relevance to Meridian:** Nearly identical scenario — inheriting an incomplete vendor project, remediating defects, establishing test coverage, and delivering documentation for handoff.

## Engagement 2 — Demand Forecasting & Restocking Dashboard

**Client:** European electronics components distributor (3 warehouses across EU)
**Duration:** 12 weeks
**Stack:** React, Node.js, Python (ML pipeline)

We designed and built a restocking recommendation engine that analyzed historical demand patterns, current inventory levels, and supplier lead times to generate optimized purchase orders within operator-defined budget constraints. The system served warehouse managers across three countries with localized interfaces (EN, DE, FR).

**Results:**
- Reduced overstock by 18% in the first quarter after deployment
- Operators reported 60% faster restocking decisions compared to manual spreadsheet process
- Multi-language interface adopted by all three warehouse teams within the first month

**Relevance to Meridian:** Direct experience building the exact type of restocking recommendation capability described in R2, including budget-constrained optimization and multi-warehouse, multi-language support.

## Engagement 3 — Legacy Dashboard Modernization & Design System

**Client:** US-based manufacturing supply chain company
**Duration:** 8 weeks
**Stack:** Vue 3, Tailwind CSS, FastAPI

The client's operations dashboard had a functional but dated interface that warehouse floor staff found difficult to use, particularly on tablets and in low-light environments. We delivered a complete UI modernization including a custom design system, dark mode for warehouse floor stations, and responsive layouts optimized for both desktop and tablet use.

**Results:**
- User satisfaction scores increased from 3.2 to 4.6 out of 5
- Dark mode adopted by 70% of warehouse floor operators within two weeks
- Design system enabled the client's internal team to build new views without external support
- Zero regressions during rollout thanks to comprehensive E2E test suite

**Relevance to Meridian:** Demonstrates our ability to deliver the "wow factor" UI refresh (D1), dark mode (D3), and a design system that Meridian can maintain long-term — all within a similar industrial/warehouse context.
