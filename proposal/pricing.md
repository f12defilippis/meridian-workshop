# Pricing

**RFP #:** MC-2026-0417 — Inventory Dashboard Modernization

---

## Pricing Model

We propose a **Time & Materials (T&M) engagement with a Not-to-Exceed (NTE) cap**, providing Meridian with both flexibility and cost predictability. This model allows us to adapt scope within phases based on findings (e.g., additional defects discovered during the Reports audit) without requiring change orders, while the NTE cap ensures total cost remains bounded.

---

## Not-to-Exceed Cap

**NTE: $140,000**

The NTE cap includes a 10% contingency buffer above the base estimate to accommodate unforeseen complexity — particularly in the Reports audit (R1), where the full defect count is unknown until the audit is complete. If work is completed under the NTE, Meridian is billed only for actual hours worked.

---

## What's Included

- All deliverables specified in §3 of the RFP (R1–R4, D1–D3)
- Weekly progress reports and milestone reviews
- Architecture documentation and test suite
- Knowledge transfer session with Meridian IT
- 2 weeks of post-engagement support for defect resolution (bugs introduced by our work only)

## What's Not Included

- Infrastructure or hosting changes
- Database migration (current system uses JSON file storage; migration to a production database is out of scope but can be scoped separately)
- Ongoing maintenance beyond the 2-week post-engagement window

---

## Payment Terms

- Invoiced bi-weekly based on actual hours worked
- Net 30 payment terms
- Phase 1 invoice due upon contract signature as a mobilization payment

---

## Assumptions

- Meridian provides timely access to stakeholders for feedback sessions and milestone reviews
- No budget range was specified by Meridian; this estimate reflects a fair market rate for the scope and complexity of the work
- The NTE cap covers all required and desired items; if Meridian elects to descope desired items (D1–D3), the estimate would reduce accordingly
